Core Features

Built for crop monitoring, result interpretation, and field reporting

Image Upload and Analysis

Users can upload crop images and receive RGB-based vegetation analysis designed to support field inspection and monitoring.

Heatzone Visualization

The system generates heatzone outputs that help users locate weak, stressed, or uneven portions of the field more quickly.

Segment-Level Review

Detailed result pages allow crop-area selection, segment comparison, and recommendations tied to specific sections of the image.

Report Generation

AgriVision exports printable PDF summaries for presentation, record-keeping, and thesis demonstration use.

Workflow

Simple upload-to-report process

  1. Register or sign in to AgriVision.
  2. Upload a crop image for analysis.
  3. Review the heatzone, summary metrics, and recommendations.
  4. Open the detailed result page for crop-area and segment inspection.
  5. Save notes and export a PDF report.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is AgriVision?

AgriVision is a web-based crop monitoring platform that interprets RGB field images to support practical field review and crop health assessment.

What kind of images can be used?

The platform is designed for standard RGB crop images, including drone captures and field imagery prepared for vegetation review.

Does AgriVision provide downloadable reports?

Yes. The website can generate PDF reports that summarize results, metrics, and recommendations.

AgriVision is intended as a crop monitoring and decision-support platform using RGB image analysis.

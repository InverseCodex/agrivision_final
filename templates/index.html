Common Questions

I uploaded an image but can’t continue. What should I do?

Make sure your file is JPG, PNG, or WEBP and smaller than 10 MB. Then try again.

Where do I see the analysis after upload?

Open the View Results tab. Your newest item should appear at the top.

Can I keep a copy of the report?

Yes. In View Results, open your result and use the PDF button to download a copy.
